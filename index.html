About Truly Kenya Digital

Truly Kenya Digital is Kenya’s leading ethical social media platform. We empower citizens at home and across the diaspora to create, share, and earn responsibly through educational, inspirational, and informative content—all powered by secure blockchain technology.

Our Core Values

Ethical Engagement

Combating misinformation and fostering national pride through responsible, uplifting content.

Digital Empowerment

Equipping users with tools to monetize creativity and participate in the global digital economy.

Monetization for All

Blockchain‑powered rewards—1 point equals 1 dollar—redeemable through M‑PESA, banks, or in‑app purchases.

Youth & Diaspora

Bridging Kenya’s vibrant youth with the global diaspora to celebrate and support local talent.

Earn as You Engage

Every like, share, and survey response turns into real value. Earn points that convert directly to Kenyan shillings, support your favourite creators, or unlock premium educational content.

  • 1 point = 1 USD equivalent
  • Powered by decentralized ImagineBC infrastructure
  • Cash‑out via M‑PESA & bank wallets

One Kenya, Stronger Together

Truly Kenya Digital unites citizens and the diaspora through stories, music, art, and civic dialogue—showcasing Kenya’s brilliance on a global stage.
